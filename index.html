About Me

Hi, I’m Iñaki Sardina — a former collegiate pitcher turned baseball analyst, currently working with the University of Miami baseball team. My work focuses on developing advanced performance models, designing intuitive data visualizations, and integrating analytics into both player development and in-game strategy. I build tools that not only evaluate performance but also translate complex data into insights coaches and decision-makers can act on immediately.

Beyond my technical work, I’m passionate about sports strategy and innovation in player evaluation. I enjoy bridging the gap between traditional baseball knowledge and modern analytics, whether it’s refining scouting reports, building predictive models, or uncovering hidden value in player performance. My long-term goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of how data can shape the game—helping front offices and coaching staffs make smarter, more informed decisions.
